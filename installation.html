🚨 Troubleshooting Common Issues

❓ EA Not Appearing in Navigator

Make sure you placed the .ex5 file in the correct Experts folder and restarted MT5 completely. Check that the file is not blocked by Windows (right-click → Properties → Unblock). Also verify the file extension is .ex5, not .ex4.

❓ AutoTrading Not Working

Enable DLL imports in MT5 settings (Tools → Options → Expert Advisors → Allow DLL imports). Also check that your broker allows Expert Advisors - most do, but some have restrictions. Ensure the "AutoTrading" button in MT5 is green.

❓ EA Not Placing Trades

Check that you have sufficient margin, trading is enabled for the symbol, and the market is open. Verify EA settings are correct and risk parameters are appropriate for your account size. Check the Experts tab for error messages.

❓ Error 133 or 138

Error 133 means invalid stops - adjust your stop loss/take profit levels. Error 138 means requote - increase slippage settings or trade during less volatile times. These are common with volatile market conditions.

❓ EA Shows Smiley Face But No Trades

The smiley face indicates EA is working but waiting for conditions. Check that your currency pair is supported, timeframes are correct, and market conditions match the EA's strategy. Test on demo account first.
